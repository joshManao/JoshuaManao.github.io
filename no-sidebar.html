Arduino based Energy Meter

Electrically powered equipment can be a pricey monthly expense for many households and businesses. Getting on top of utility stage, especially electricity, which typically constitutes the largest part of the monthly expenses. Accurate energy bills are important factor managing the household finances especially in these difficult times. Without regular meter readings, the data collected is not secured and can be manipulated when it transmit to the electric company. Figuring out how much we’re using and where it’s all going, one method of doing this is to have simple home energy meter powered by Arduino. It is simple yet effective system that will verify and check energy usage accurately. This proposed project is perfect match if we want to save money, to monitor and limit our energy consumption in kilo-watt per hour.
